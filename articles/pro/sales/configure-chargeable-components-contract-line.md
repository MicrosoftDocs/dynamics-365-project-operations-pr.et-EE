<span data-ttu-id="e2498-105">Projektipõhine lepingurida *sisaldas* komponente ja *arveldatavaid* komponente.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="e2498-105">A project-based contract line has *included* components and *chargeable* components.</span></span>

### <a name="update-a-project-task-as-chargeable-or-non-chargeable"></a><span data-ttu-id="e2498-121">Projekti tööülesande värskendamine arveldatavaks või mittearveldatavaks</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="e2498-121">Update a project task as chargeable or non-chargeable</span></span>
