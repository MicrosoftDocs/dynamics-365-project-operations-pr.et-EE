### <a name="update-a-project-task-to-be-chargeable-or-non-chargeable"></a><span data-ttu-id="4b9b1-121">Projekti tööülesande värskendamine arveldatavaks või mittearveldatavaks</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="4b9b1-121">Update a project task to be chargeable or non-chargeable</span></span>
